Privacy & Data Policy

1. Introduction

SofaFlix is a free Android app that streams movies/series, manages a local library, and shows non‑intrusive ads.

2. Information We Collect

None (personal data)No email, login, usage logs, device IDsService operates without personal data

Ads load in fixed‑size WebView blocks. The request contains only the page identifier and dimensions; no identifying data is sent.

6. Your Rights

Clear all data via Android Settings → Apps → SofaFlix → Storage → Clear storage, or uninstall the app.

7. Security

All traffic uses HTTPS; data stays inside the app’s private sandbox.
