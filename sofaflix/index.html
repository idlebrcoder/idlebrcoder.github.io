SofaFlix

Free app to watch movies and series and manage your library.

What it does

  • Stream movies and TV series at no cost.
  • Organize titles into personal collections.
  • Search.

Key features

  • Navigation optimized for android tv, but also works fine with touch and click.
  • Automatic metadata fetching (posters, descriptions).
  • Export/import library data as JSON.
  • Privacy‑focused.
  • No telemetry.
  • No account.
